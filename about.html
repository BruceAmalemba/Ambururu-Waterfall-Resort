Our Timeless Journey

Sacred Luo Site

Ambururu Waterfalls, revered by the Luo community, served as a spiritual haven where ancestors connected with nature along the Yala River.

Natural Wonder Formed

Centuries of Yala River erosion crafted the majestic cascades, establishing Ambururu as a landmark in Musanda.

Vision Takes Root

Justice Anthony Ombwayo envisioned a sustainable resort to preserve Ambururu’s beauty, blending luxury with eco-tourism.

Global Destination

Ambururu Waterfall Resort opens, earning a 4.3/5 Google Maps rating and becoming a premier eco-tourism hub.

Our Visionary Leader

Justice Anthony Ombwayo

A distinguished Kenyan judge and ardent conservationist, Justice Anthony Ombwayo transformed Ambururu into a world-class eco-resort. His passion for Siaya’s natural beauty and Luo heritage drives a mission of sustainable tourism, empowering local communities while offering guests an unforgettable experience.

Rooted in Siaya, Justice Ombwayo’s legal career is matched by his dedication to environmental and cultural preservation. The resort employs over 50 locals and supports initiatives like tree planting and artisan crafts.

Building a Brighter Future

Environmental Stewardship

Community Empowerment

Cultural Celebration

Promoting Luo traditions through art, festivals, and local crafts.
